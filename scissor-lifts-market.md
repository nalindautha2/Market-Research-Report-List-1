<p><p>The scissor lift market primarily comprises three types: hydraulic, pneumatic, and mechanical scissor lifts. Hydraulic scissor lifts use fluid pressure to raise and lower platforms, offering high stability and lifting power, ideal for heavy loads. Pneumatic scissor lifts rely on compressed air for operation, providing lightweight and portable options for quick maneuverability. Mechanical scissor lifts utilize mechanical systems, often employing gears or pulleys, which can be more straightforward but may require more physical effort and maintenance.</p></p>
