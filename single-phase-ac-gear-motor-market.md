<p><strong>The Single-Phase AC Gear-Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Asynchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Single-Phase AC Gear-Motor market is primarily categorized into two types: synchronous and asynchronous motors. Synchronous motors operate at a speed that is directly related to the frequency of the AC supply, ensuring precise speed control and high efficiency, making them ideal for applications requiring constant speed. In contrast, asynchronous motors, also known as induction motors, operate at a speed that varies with load and are widely used for their robustness and simplicity, often found in general-purpose applications.</p></p>
