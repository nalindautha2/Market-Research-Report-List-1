<p><strong>The 3D Electrophysiology Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Electrophysiology Navigation System market is divided into two main types: Contact Type and Non-Contact Type. Contact Type systems require physical contact with the heart tissue, utilizing catheters to map electrical activity, ensuring precise localization of arrhythmias. In contrast, Non-Contact Type systems operate without direct contact, using external sensors and magnetic fields to create 3D maps of cardiac electrical signals, allowing for comprehensive visualization and analysis. Both types enhance the accuracy and effectiveness of electrophysiological procedures, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
