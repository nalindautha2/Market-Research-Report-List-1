<p><p>The industrial robot servo motor market consists of two main types: AC servo motors and DC servo motors. AC servo motors are preferred for their high efficiency, durability, and ability to maintain consistent torque at varying speeds, making them ideal for precise and high-performance applications. In contrast, DC servo motors offer simpler control and lower cost, making them suitable for smaller, less demanding robotic applications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing automation and productivity in manufacturing environments.</p></p>
