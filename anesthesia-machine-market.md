<p><p>The anesthesia machine market is divided into two primary types: trolley-mounted and table-mounted models. Trolley-mounted anesthesia machines are designed for mobility, equipped with wheels for easy transport within hospitals or surgical facilities, allowing flexibility in different operating rooms. In contrast, table-mounted anesthesia machines are fixed units that are securely placed near the surgical table, providing a stable and permanent solution for administering anesthesia during procedures. Both types cater to varying operational needs in medical settings, enhancing patient care and surgical efficiency.</p></p>
