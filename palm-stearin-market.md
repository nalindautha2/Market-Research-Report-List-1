<p><p>The palm stearin market is categorized into two main types: soft stearin and hard stearin. Soft stearin, derived from palm oil, has a lower melting point and is primarily used in food products, confectionery, and margarine. In contrast, hard stearin has a higher melting point and is suitable for industrial applications, including cosmetics and candles. Both types are valued for their stability, cost-effectiveness, and versatility, catering to various consumer and industrial needs in the market.</p></p>
