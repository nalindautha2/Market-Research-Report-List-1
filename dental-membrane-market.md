<p><strong>The Dental Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resorbable Dental Membrane</li><li>Non-resorbable Dental Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental membrane market comprises two main types: resorbable and non-resorbable membranes. Resorbable dental membranes are designed to break down and be absorbed by the body over time, promoting tissue regeneration while eliminating the need for surgical removal. Non-resorbable membranes, on the other hand, are created from durable materials that provide long-term support for tissue engineering but require removal after use. Both types are crucial in guided tissue regeneration and dental implant procedures, enhancing healing and stability.</p></p>
